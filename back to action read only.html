A chiropractor specializes in diagnosing and treating problems with the musculoskeletal system, including your joints, muscles, ligaments, and the nerves that connect everything. The primary tool is the chiropractic adjustment: a precise, controlled force applied to a joint that's restricted or misaligned, restoring proper movement and nervous system function. At Back to Action, adjustments are paired with soft tissue therapy using the PMR 1000 percussion machine, Game Ready cold compression, and NormaTec recovery. A full performance toolkit, not just a table and a crack.

Conditions we commonly treat: back pain, neck pain, headaches, sciatica, IT band syndrome, rotator cuff strains, shoulder impingement, frozen shoulder, golfer's and tennis elbow, runner's knee, ankle sprains, bursitis, tendonitis, postural issues, and sports performance optimization.

Chiropractic works for a wide range of people, from weekend warriors and competitive athletes to office workers, seniors, and kids. If you're dealing with pain, limited mobility, or recurring injuries, you're likely a great candidate. If for any reason chiropractic isn't the right fit, Dr. Hanstad will tell you honestly and refer you out.

Your first visit runs about 60 minutes. Here's what to expect: a thorough health history review, a physical exam covering posture, range of motion, and muscle strength, and a clear explanation of what's going on and how we plan to address it.
